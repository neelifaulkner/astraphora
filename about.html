About Astraphora

Astraphora is a quiet corner of the internet—an intentional space to pause, breathe, and speak to yourself with gentleness. Each affirmation is a small star to navigate by.

The Vision

In a noisy world, Astraphora offers a minimal, moody sanctuary for mindful manifestation. One line at a time, it invites you to return to what’s steady: breath, presence, and inner knowing.

The Name

Astraphora evokes the feeling of bearing stars—carrying light through darkness, and letting that light guide you home.

The Creator

Created by Neeli Faulkner, investigative storyteller and founder of Murder Creek Media. Astraphora is a personal practice turned public tool: a way to make space for clarity, courage, and soft expansion.
